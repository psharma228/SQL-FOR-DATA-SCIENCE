Q8 Show the number of orders placed by each customer and sort the result by the number of orders in descending order.
SELECT CustomerId, COUNT(*) AS Orders
FROM Invoices
GROUP BY CustomerId
ORDER BY Orders DESC

What is the number of items bought for the 8th person on the list?
7

Q9 Find the albums with 12 or more tracks.
